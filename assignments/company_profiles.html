Job Market Research¶

The job hunt is approaching quickly and it helps to have a heading on what to look for, wherever you’re looking for it. Spend some time this weekend doing research.

Tasks¶

  • Find five (5) companies in a city where you want to work (whether it’s Seattle or elsewhere) that interest you for some reason and either are hiring or have hired developers that can write Python.

  • For each company answer the following:
    • What is it that they do?
    • Why does this company interest you?
    • How large is the company (if that info is available)
    • Why are they hiring developers that can write Python?

Submission¶

Compile your answers to these questions and submit them as a text submission to Canvas. Each question should need no more than 5 sentences to answer, but if you really must be verbose don’t go over 10. I’m not trying to read an essay.
