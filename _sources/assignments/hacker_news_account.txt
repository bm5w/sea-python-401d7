*********************************
Sign Up for a Hacker News Account
*********************************

.. slide:: Sign Up for a Hacker News Account
    :level: 1

    This document contains no slides.

News aggregation sites like Hacker News can expose you to the latest
information about your new field. In addition, establishing a reputation by
voting and commenting on stories in a concientious fashion can help you be more
visible in the community.

Remember when participating in any discussion that you are a representative of
Code Fellows. Behave accordingly.

Tasks
=====

Sign up for Hacker news: https://news.ycombinator.com/

Upvote some articles you like, comment on a few too.

By establishing a history of commenting and voting, you increase your sway in
the system.  Then, when a classmate's technical blog hits, you can use your
power to push it toward the front page.

Be aware that HN has a limit on the number of new users who can sign up in any
given time period, so you may need to try again in a day or two.  Keep at it,
eventually you'll be allowed to join.
