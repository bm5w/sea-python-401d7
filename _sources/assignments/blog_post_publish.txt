Technical Blog Post: Publish Your Post
**************************************

.. slide:: Technical Blog Post: Publish Your Post
    :level: 1

    This document contains no slides.

In this series of assignments, you will be writing a blog post to share something you have learned with those who may be interested in following a similar path.

You began by generating some ideas.
Then you picked an idea and generated an outline.
Then you wrote a rough draft from that outline.
Now you will finalize and publish your post.

Tasks
=====

Integrate peer-review feedback and polish up your blog post for final publishing.
Double-check for grammar and spelling errors.
Ensure code examples are nicely syntax-highlighted.
Read it out loud at least once.

Submitting Your Work
====================

When your post is ready, hit "Publish" and submit the link in canvas.
Then share your post on Twitter and the like for wider community feedback.
Woo!

In lieu of peer review, watch for classmates tweeting about their own posts, and leave some kind comments for them. :]
